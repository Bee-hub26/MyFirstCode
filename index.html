🐟 Citrus Lime Fish Ceviche
Ingredients: Fresh fish fillets, lime juice, red onion, cilantro, fresh chili.
Dice the fresh fish into clean cubes. Marinate completely in fresh lime juice until the fish turns opaque. Toss gently with finely sliced red onions, fresh cilantro, and a hint of chili for an incredible kick!
